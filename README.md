# 🚛 Fleet Management Analysis

A comprehensive dashboard analyzing fleet performance, operational costs, vehicle efficiency, and freight activity across truck types, trailer categories, states, and time periods to identify trends, optimize logistics strategies, and support data-driven business decisions.

---

# 📋 Project Requirements

The analysis was performed using data imported from multiple sources containing fleet operations, freight records, cost breakdowns, vehicle details, and driver information.

- **Data Cleaning:** Removed duplicates, corrected inconsistencies, and handled missing values.

- **Data Integration:** Combined multiple datasets, including freight, vehicle, driver, cost, and calendar tables.

- **Analytical Readiness:** Prepared metrics for net revenue, total cost, profit, cost per ton, cost per mile, fuel cost, maintenance cost, and freight counts.

- **Dashboarding:** Created interactive dashboards to explore trends by truck type, trailer type, state, driver, month, and quarter.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

The logistics company has experienced significant variations in revenue, profit, costs, and freight activity over the reported period (2018–2019). Performance differs across truck types, trailer categories, states, and individual drivers. Cost structures vary between fixed and variable components, and freight volume fluctuates seasonally. A clear understanding of these patterns is required to optimize fleet utilization, reduce operational costs, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🧐 Key Business Questions

- Which **truck types and trailer categories** generate the highest revenue and profit, and which may require operational review or cost optimization?
- How is **net revenue distributed across states**, and which regions show potential for increased freight activity or market expansion?
- What are the trends in **freight volume on a monthly and quarterly basis**, and which periods show the greatest growth opportunities?
- How do **fixed and variable costs fluctuate over time**, and what is the breakdown between fuel, maintenance, and other cost components?
- Which **drivers incur the highest costs**, and how can driver performance and cost efficiency be improved?
- How do **cost per ton and cost per mile** compare across truck types, and which vehicle categories achieve better cost efficiency?
- What is the **profit margin per truck type**, and how can fleet composition be optimized to maximize overall profitability?

# 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Fleet Performance Overview
Tracks overall KPIs including Net Revenue ($484.53M), Total Cost ($3.77M), Weight (664.17K tons), and Total Freights (5K). Includes revenue and cost trends over time and net revenue breakdown by state.

### 2. Fleet Costs
Analyzes cost structure including Fuel Cost ($901.89K), Maintenance Cost ($267.41K), Cost Per Ton ($5.68), and Cost Per Mile ($5.16). Features fixed vs. variable cost trends, top 5 drivers by cost, and cost breakdown by trailer type and truck type.

### 3. Vehicle Analysis
Deep-dives into vehicle-level performance, including Profit (480.76M), Distance (731.57K miles), Weight carried, and number of trucks used (23). Includes profit trends over time, profit by trailer type (Reefer, Fridge, Dry), and a detailed breakdown table by truck type.
